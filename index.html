01 — About

A scientist who builds things

I'm a data scientist specialising in machine learning for banking and finance — building systems that don't just train well, but hold up in the real world.

My work spans the full ML lifecycle: feature engineering, model training with XGBoost and LightGBM, deployment via FastAPI, and live monitoring with Evidently and MLflow. I believe a model without a monitoring dashboard is a model waiting to fail silently.

Beyond ML, I bring geospatial expertise through QGIS and GPS systems, build full-stack web applications, and create AI-powered tools that automate complex workflows. Based in Dar es Salaam — working globally.
